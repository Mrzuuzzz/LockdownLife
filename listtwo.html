Survival Guide for Quarantine

Time just keeps passing by and you’re not sure what to do. This deadly boredom is getting to you! Luckily, you found this Quarantine Survival Guide, and we can try to help you find some things to do.

  • Stock up on masks for when you’ll need them
  • Have hand sanitizer on you at all times to sanitize your hands and belongings
  • Buy out all the toilet paper in Costco (just kidding, stock up on some but don’t go overboard)
  • Stock up on necessary supplies
  • Learn how to make sourdough bread
  • Find ways to contact your friends and family
  • Make a schedule for school and your assignments
  • Create a 372 song Spotify playlist
  • Find personal time
  • Gain a new appreciation for baking
  • Binge watch shows on Netflix
  • Watch that one anime series all your friends have been begging you to watch
  • Learn an instrument
  • Organize a game night with friends over a call
  • Read some books and/or stories
  • Spend your time wisely on all your work
  • Create a 10 gigabyte meme folder
  • Join a group you’re interested in
  • Improve your stick men drawings
