Covid-19 Info

CDC Covid 19 Site

The official CDC Website for Covid-19 acts as a guidebook. You may have questions about the virus or you may be researching it, but this website can give you the necessary information.

WHO Website

The World Health Organization (WHO) website offers much more information. Advice, research, news, and vaccination procedures can also be their site.

The New York Times Graph

People should collectively take necessary precautions to stay safe from the virus. Make sure to remind people of some things that they can do like wearing a mask, washing their hands, and social distancing.
